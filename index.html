The best waffles ever (that I found off the internet)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • ~1 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 6 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanila

Steps:

  1. Wisk flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl. Set aside. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. Beat eggs in a separate bowl, then stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the flour mixture and mix.
  4. Latle batter into the waffle iron and cook until it's reached your desired crispiness (at least 3 min)
  5. Serve with butter, wipped cream, or syrup. Enjoy!
